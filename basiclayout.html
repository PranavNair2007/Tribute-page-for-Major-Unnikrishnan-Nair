Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan

Last word:"Do not come up, I will handle them."

personal life

  • Birth- he was borm on March 15, 1977, in Kozhikode, Kerala.
  • Family- He was the only child of K. Unnikrishnan, a retired ISRO officer, and Dhanalakshmi Unnikrishnan. He was raised in Bangalore, where his family settled.
  • Education He spent 14 years at The Frank Anthony Public School in Bangalore, graduating in 1995. Even as a student, he was known for his "crew cut" hairstyle, signaling his lifelong dream of joining the military.
  • Passions Outside of academics, he was an exceptional athlete and a member of the school choir. He was also a self-proclaimed "movie buff."

Military Career & Valor

  • 1999 - Commissioned into the 7th Battalion of the Bihar Regiment; served in Operation Vijay (Kargil).
  • 2007 - Joined the elite 51 Special Action Group of the National Security Guard (NSG).
  • Nov 2008 - Led the rescue operation at the Taj Mahal Palace Hotel during the Mumbai terror attacks.
  • Ultimate Sacrifice - Saved his colleagues and hostages, engaging terrorists single-handedly until his last breath.
  • Honors - Posthumously awarded the Ashoka Chakra, India's highest peacetime gallantry award.
